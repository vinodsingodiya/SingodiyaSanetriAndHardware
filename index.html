About Us Namaskar India Tour

Namaskar India Tour is one of the leading inbound tourism companies that focuses on designing the most amazing, custom-made tours through the colorful Indian and Nepalese scenery. Located in the culturally diversified state of Rajasthan in Northern India, we specialize in offering custom-designed travel opportunities to Individual Travelers (FIT), Group Inclusive Tours (GIT) and tailor-made itinerary to meet the specific needs and preferences of every traveler.

Under the leadership of a renowned travel professional, who has the unmatched love affair with India, Mr. Balbeer Singh, Namaskar India Tour has grown to become a reputable tourism company. Mr. Singh has more than 10 years in the tourism sector and is considered as an innovator in the Indian tourism industry who possesses cross-industry knowledge and good access to networks that add value to every trip that we provide.

The purpose behind our commitment to excellence and the need to create memorable travel experiences within India, Nepal and Bhutan is our

hardworking team of more than 10 travel gurus who serve 24 hours a day. Namaskar India Tour is on a serious takeoff under the effective leadership of our managing directors and has bagged many accolades and industry recognition to our name.

We are very proud of the fact that we have received the TripAdvisor Certificate of Excellence in 2018 and 2019, which is a confirmation of the quality of the services that we offer. We are also the proud owners of the Indian Association of Tour Operators (IATO) and the Ecotourism Society of India with formal recognition by the Ministry of Tourism, Government of India.

Having a long history of quality services in the travel business, Namaskar India Tour has been proud to accommodate more than 10,000 satisfied customers throughout the world. We are of the opinion that every trip should be a chance to make memorable moments and unique experiences that will last even after the trip is over. We are about to discover the grandeur, the varieties of culture and richness of India, where there is no corner without a story that will be discovered.
